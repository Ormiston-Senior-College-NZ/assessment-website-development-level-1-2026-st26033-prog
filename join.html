Join the Club

The Club of Indian Diaspora welcomes all students at Ormiston Senior College. You do not need to be Indian to join. Anyone who is interested in culture, music, dance, and festivals is welcome.

Opportunities

  • Participate in cultural performances
  • Help organise school events and festivals
  • Develop leadership and teamwork skills
  • Learn about Indian traditions and history
